# Privacy Policy for Spark It!

**Last Updated: October 30, 2025**

---

## Introduction

Welcome to Spark It! ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your relationship. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

**By using Spark It!, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information:**
- Partner names (customizable display names)
- Phone numbers (optional, for SMS notifications)
- Email addresses (for account recovery and notifications)
- Location (city/state, optional, for AI-powered local activity suggestions)
- Relationship preferences (long-distance, polyamorous, etc.)

**Subscription Information:**
- Payment details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
- Subscription tier (Free Trial, Monthly, or Yearly)
- Purchase history and billing information

**Activity Data:**
- Activity ratings and preferences
- Spark counter (number of activities generated)
- Scoreboard data (wins, ties, activity completion)
- Trivia contest results and answers
- Custom avatar selections (premium users)
- Uploaded avatar images (stored securely in object storage, premium users only)

**Communications:**
- Messages sent through our support channels (support@spark-itapp.com)
- Feedback and survey responses

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Information:**
- App features used (button presses, trivia challenges, video calls)
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- IP address and approximate location (for fraud prevention)
- Session duration and frequency of use
- Browser type and version (for web app users)

**Push Notification Data:**
- Browser push notification subscriptions
- Notification preferences and delivery status

**Video Calling Data:**
- Video session duration and connection quality (via Daily.co integration)
- No video or audio content is recorded or stored by us

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

**Payment Processor (Stripe):**
- Payment confirmation and subscription status
- We do not receive or store your full credit card details

**SMS Service (Twilio):**
- Phone number verification status
- SMS delivery confirmation

**AI Service (OpenAI via Replit):**
- Location data sent to generate local activity suggestions
- No personally identifiable information is sent to AI services

**Video Service (Daily.co):**
- Video call connection metadata
- Video/audio streams are end-to-end encrypted and not stored

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Core Service Delivery
- **Facilitate the simultaneous button press experience** between you and your partner
- **Generate personalized activity suggestions** based on your preferences and location
- **Enable real-time notifications** (SMS and browser push) when your partner presses the button
- **Provide trivia challenges** and maintain competitive scoreboards
- **Support video calling** for long-distance couples
- **Track your free trial** (10 sparks or 7 days) and subscription status

### 2.2 Account Management
- Create and manage your couple account
- Process payments and subscriptions through Stripe
- Send transactional emails (signup confirmations, password resets, receipts)
- Provide customer support

### 2.3 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
- Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user behavior
- Conduct internal research and analytics

### 2.4 Communication
- Send important service updates and security alerts
- Notify you about subscription renewals and billing
- Respond to your support requests
- Send promotional emails (you can opt out anytime)

### 2.5 Legal and Safety
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect the rights and safety of our users

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties.

- Protect our legal rights

- Respond to emergencies involving danger to persons

### 3.3 Business Transfers

If Spark It! is acquired, merged, or sold, your information may be transferred to the new owner. We will notify you via email and app notification before this happens.

### 3.4 With Your Consent

We may share information with other parties when you give us explicit consent to do so.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

**Account Data:**
- Retained while your account is active
- Deleted within 90 days of account deletion (unless required by law)

**Activity Ratings & Results:**
- Retained for 2 years to improve recommendations
- Anonymized after account deletion

**Payment Records:**
- Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
- Stored securely by Stripe

**Support Communications:**
- Retained for 3 years for quality assurance

**Logs and Analytics:**
- Retained for 1 year, then automatically deleted

**Video Call Metadata:**
- Deleted within 30 days (no video/audio is stored)

You can request early deletion of your data by contacting support@spark-itapp.com.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format (JSON)
- Contact: privacy@spark-itapp.com

### 5.2 Correction and Update
You can:
- Update partner names and preferences in Settings
- Change your email or phone number
- Modify location information
- Contact us to correct inaccurate data

### 5.3 Deletion
You can:
- Delete your account anytime through Settings
- Request complete data deletion via privacy@spark-itapp.com
- We will delete your data within 90 days (except legal retention requirements)

### 5.4 Opt-Out Rights

**Email Marketing:**
- Unsubscribe link in every promotional email
- Transactional emails (receipts, security alerts) cannot be disabled

**SMS Notifications:**
- Disable in app Settings
- Reply STOP to any SMS message
- Note: This may affect core app functionality (partner notifications)

**Browser Push Notifications:**
- Revoke permission in your browser settings
- Disable in app Settings

**AI Features:**
- AI activity suggestions are optional
- Can be disabled by removing location data

### 5.5 Region-Specific Rights

**For EU Residents (GDPR):**
- Right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data

**For California Residents (CCPA):**
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of "sale" of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Contact: privacy@spark-itapp.com

**For Other Regions:**
- We honor privacy rights regardless of location
- Contact: privacy@spark-itapp.com

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- Bcrypt password hashing (10 rounds)
- Secure session management with PostgreSQL storage
- Object storage encryption for uploaded avatars
- Regular security audits and updates

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Access controls limiting employee access to data
- Regular staff training on privacy and security
- Incident response procedures
- Secure development practices

**Third-Party Security:**
- All service providers undergo security assessments
- SOC 2 compliant partners (Stripe, Twilio, Daily.co)
- End-to-end encryption for video calls

**Limitations:**
No security system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take all reasonable measures to protect your data.

## 12. Special Disclosures

### 12.1 Couples Data

Your data is shared with your partner by design:
- Both partners can see activity ratings
- Both partners see scoreboard data
- Both partners receive notifications

**Account Deletion:**
If one partner deletes the account, all couple data is deleted for both partners.

### 12.2 Free Trial Tracking

We track your free trial usage:
- Number of sparks used (max 10)
- Days since signup (max 7)
- Whichever limit is reached first ends the trial

This data is used solely for trial enforcement and is not shared with third parties.

### 12.3 Video Calling Privacy

**Daily.co Integration:**
- Video and audio are **not recorded or stored** by us or Daily.co
- Calls are end-to-end encrypted
- Session metadata (duration, quality) is logged for 30 days
- No call content is accessible to Spark It! staff

### 12.4 AI Activity Suggestions

**OpenAI Integration:**
- Only your city/state is sent to OpenAI (no names or personal data)
- AI-generated activities are not stored long-term
- You can disable this feature by removing location data
- Feature is only active when enabled by environment variables

### 12.5 Polyamorous Relationships

For users in polyamorous/ENM relationships:
- We only support couple accounts (2 partners)
- No data about other relationships is collected
- Polyamorous activity suggestions do not imply knowledge of other partners
